Our Policies

Financing

1.Financing is only available for appliances that consume in Durham House of Pets


2.The applicant must be an active DHP customer in good standing.


3.The applicant must complete and sign the credit application in order for DHP to obtain credit information


4.DHP will allow financing for to up 12 months, with monthly installment payments added to the applicant’s bill. Financing in excess of 12 months may be allowed at the discretion of the President.


5.Credit approval will be based on: (a) at least 12 months satisfactory payment history as a DPH customer, or (b) a Beacon score (or equivalent) of at least 600 on the applicant’s credit report.


6.Maximum credit allowed is $2,000.


Delivery

1.General information

All orders are subject to product availability. If an item is not in stock at the time you place your order, we will notify you and refund you the total amount of your order, using the original method of payment.

2.Delivery Location

Items offered on our website are only available for delivery to addresses in US. [We also accept orders from international customers who are shipping to addresses in UK,Australia only.Any shipments outside of Japan are not available at this time.

3.Delivery Time

An estimated delivery time will be provided to you once your order is placed. Delivery times are estimates and commence from the date of shipping, rather than the date of order. Delivery times are to be used as a guide only and are subject to the acceptance and approval of your order. Unless there are exceptional circumstances, we make every effort to fulfill your order within [15] business days of the date of your order. Business day mean Monday to Friday, except holidays.

4.Damaged Items in Transport

If there is any damage to the packaging on delivery, contact us immediately .

5.Questions

If you have any questions about the delivery and shipment or your order, please contact us .

Return Policies

Online Purchase-Return Policy

Sale and discounted items can be returned for a merchandise credit within 30 days of the shipping date. For all returns, items must not be washed, worn or damaged. All original tags must be attached. International return shipping costs are not covered by Durham House of Pets.

Gift Return Policy

Well, this is awkward. If you received a gift that you’d like to return please contact Customer Care for help. You can return the item within 30 days of the shipping date, or exchange it for a merchandise credit within 40 days of the shipping date.

Online Return Refund

Refunds will be credited to the original method of payment via Customer Car All returned items that were purchased using a gift card will be refunded back onto a gift card. All refunds will be processed within 7 to 10 business days upon receipt of your return package.

Order Cancellation

Hit the "Submit Order" button too soon? We’ll do our best to help. During Customer Care hours only, you have up to 30 minutes from the time you click “Submit Order” to cancel it by Contacting Us.
